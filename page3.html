Review

During my time in Hawaii this summer, I spent two days at Honolulu and four in Maui. In Honolulu, my family and I relaxed at the beach and saw the Honolulu Zoo. In Maui, I went on a sunset cruise, took a surfing lesson, and went to a Luau. We also went to amazing restaurants all nights. Overall, Hawaii was definitely my favorite vacation and I will always remember the memories I made there.
